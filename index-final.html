Our mission

We are a small animal welfare charity located in Sydney’s Western Suburbs who care deeply about our furry friends who are not able to care for themselves. It is our mission to ensure that the homeless, abandoned, neglected and abused have a safe haven to heal and learn to love and trust again so they can find their very own safe, loving and furrever home.

We have many programs designed to create nurturing environments for these beautiful little souls, not just in our care but to improve the lives of those living in the streets. Please read more about how you can help with our Foster to Adopt and TNR iniatives here.

Our amazing network of foster carers and volunteers have been working together since 2018, saving one animal at a time.


To date, we’ve rescued and re-homed more than 1700 companion animals!
